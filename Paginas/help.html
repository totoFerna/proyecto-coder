FAQ

SHIPPING

How do I know if they ship to my country? "Be him" ships worldwide, so don't worry and buy what you want so that you too can be Him

PAYMENT

How can I pay for my purchases? "Be him" accepts all means of payment, such as credit cards, debit cards or even paypal so this will not be a problem with your next purchase

PACKAGE

How do I know when my package will arrive? At the time of making the purchase, a tracking code will be sent to your email by which you will be able to follow the trajectory of your order in real time.

WRONG ORDER

What happens if I receive something that was not what I expected? If when you receive your package it is not what you ordered, do not worry, we will take care of removing it and correctly delivering the order at no cost. We will also give you a 20% discount code for your next purchase

TIME TO DELIVER THE ORDER

How long does it take to deliver my order? This depends on where the order was placed from, but generally orders usually take between 5-10 business days.
